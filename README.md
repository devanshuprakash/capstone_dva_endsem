# Credit Risk & Loan Default Analysis
### Newton School of Technology — Capstone 2

## Problem Statement
What borrower and loan characteristics are the strongest predictors of default, and how can a lender reduce portfolio risk without sacrificing loan volume?

## Sector
Finance — Consumer Lending / Credit Risk

## Dataset
- **Source:** Lending Club Loan Data (Kaggle)
- **Size:** ~2.2M rows, 150+ columns
- **Period:** 2007–2018
